I'm Maryam Seher — an AI Engineering student at NUTECH Islamabad, constantly building before things feel "ready." I don't really wait for perfect plans or permission. I start, figure things out in motion, and refine through execution.

My journey didn't begin in a typical way. While most were still exploring ideas, I stepped into NUTECH's incubation space with an early startup direction that got selected — not because everything was fully defined, but because the execution was real. That experience shaped how I think: clarity comes from doing, not waiting.

Alongside that, I've worked as a freelancer in email design and digital communication — learning how design, structure, and psychology influence conversion and user behavior.

Now, I'm deep into AI systems and automation — building agents, workflows, and tools that replace manual effort with intelligent execution.

From AI agents to automation pipelines, I work with tools like LangChain, n8n, VAPI, Gemini, Selenium, and WhatsApp APIs — choosing whatever solves the problem, not what's trending.

I'm still evolving — but always building.
